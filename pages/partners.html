Common questions
How do I track referrals?
We track referrals by unique code or link. You can also simply have your client mention your name at checkout — we'll match it manually and confirm with you.
When do I get paid?
Commissions are calculated monthly and paid within 15 days of month-end. Minimum payout is $50. We pay via PayPal or direct deposit.
Is there a minimum number of referrals?
No. One referral or one hundred — you earn on every single one. There are no tiers, no minimums, and no caps.
Do I earn on one-time purchases too?
Yes. For one-time products like Listicle Inclusion ($1,500), Company Spotlight ($2,500), or Full Visibility Package ($4,500), you earn a flat 20% on the purchase price.
Can I be both a partner and a customer?
Absolutely. Many of our partners also have their own company listed on our sites. You just can't refer yourself.
