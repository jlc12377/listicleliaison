Common questions
How do I track my referrals and commissions?+
When approved, you receive a unique referral link. Any client who signs up through your link is tracked to your account. We send monthly commission reports and payments via direct deposit or PayPal.
Do I need to sell or close deals?+
No. Our checkout is completely self-serve. Your job is to share the tool (ai-mentions.com), show clients their visibility score, and share your referral link. They choose their plan and pay online. You never need to get on a call or negotiate pricing.
How long do I earn commission on a referral?+
For as long as the client remains subscribed. If a client stays for 2 years on an Enhanced Listing, you earn $60/month for 24 months — that's $1,440 from a single referral.
What is the Listicle Liaison publishing network?+
We operate 7+ independent ranking and editorial sites across software, AI, crypto, casino gaming, and more. Our sites rank products using transparent methodology and are indexed by major search engines and AI assistants. When a client gets listed, they appear across the entire network.
Can I use ai-mentions.com in my client reporting?+
Absolutely. Run any client through ai-mentions.com, screenshot the results, and include it in your reports. It's a free tool — no attribution required. When clients see their AI visibility score and want to improve it, share your referral link.
Is there a minimum number of referrals required?+
No minimums. Refer one client or one hundred — the 20% commission applies to every referral regardless of volume.
