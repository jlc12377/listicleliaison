Common questions

Do I need a subscription AND an editorial package?

No — each can work independently. All our editorial packages include a 12-month directory listing equivalent to our Claimed Profile tier. If you want top-of-category placement or ongoing visibility beyond 12 months, a subscription complements the editorial package nicely but isn't required.

Can I upgrade from a subscription to an editorial package later?

Yes, and we recommend it for many businesses. Start with a Claimed Profile or Enhanced Listing to establish presence, then add a Listicle or Spotlight Feature once you have a clear story to tell. Your subscription carries over.

What's the difference between Listicle Inclusion and Spotlight Feature?

The Listicle Inclusion ($1,500) gets you a 1,500-2,500 word article that features your business among others in your category — think "Top 10 X Companies." The Spotlight Feature ($2,500) is a longer, dedicated 2,500-4,000 word profile purely about you, with homepage promotion across our network.

How do I know which category my business fits into?

Email partnerships@listicleliaison.com with a brief description of your business and we'll recommend the right site in our network. We have 11+ category-specific sites covering software, AI, healthcare, financial services, trading education, local services, and more.

How long until I see results?

Directory listings go live within 48 hours (Basic, Claimed) or 1-2 weeks (Enhanced, Featured). Editorial packages publish within 2-4 weeks depending on scope. AI citation pickup typically happens within 2-8 weeks after publication, depending on how AI systems index and cite new content.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime. Semi-annual and annual plans are committed terms but renew on your schedule. Editorial packages are one-time payments with no recurring charges — your article and directory listing stay live for the duration included.

Do you vet the businesses you feature?

Yes. Every business goes through editorial verification including business registration confirmation, website review, and category fit evaluation. Some industries (financial services, health, legal) have additional compliance steps. Businesses that don't pass verification are not listed.
