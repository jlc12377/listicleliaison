What is a listicle inclusion service?

A subscription-based service that places your company in published industry rankings, "Top 10" lists, and editorial roundups across specialized publications.

Listicle inclusion services solve a specific problem: getting your company featured in the published rankings that buyers, search engines, and AI assistants reference when evaluating solutions in your category.

Traditional PR agencies charge $5,000–$15,000 per month for outreach campaigns that may or may not result in placements. Self-service link-building platforms sell low-quality directory listings that don't move the needle. A proper listicle inclusion service sits in between — purpose-built editorial placement at predictable monthly pricing.

At Listicle Liaison, we operate 11 specialized industry publications across 8 verticals. Subscribers get featured in our published rankings with editorial profiles, permanent backlinks, schema-tagged content, and ongoing visibility maintenance. Listings go live within 2–3 weeks of subscription start.

The core difference from one-off listicle placements: subscription-based listicle inclusion compounds. Your company stays in the rankings month after month while we add new categories, refresh content, and publish new editorial coverage that points back to your listings.
