Common Questions

Things buyers ask.

How is this different from buying a Forbes contributor article?

Forbes Contributor placements typically run $1,500-5,000 per article and live on Forbes. Our placements live in industry-specific ranked listicles ($224/mo locked early-partner rate, lower per-month than a single Forbes article one-time fee), built specifically for AI citation and topical authority. Different products solving different problems — many of our customers do both.

Are the listicles ranking on Google yet?

We're a newer network, and brand new pages take 60-90 days minimum to rank in Google for competitive terms. We're transparent about this. Our pages are already indexed by AI tools (which don't have the same ranking-velocity requirement) and the topical authority compounds with time. Early customers benefit by locking in pricing before standard rates apply.

What's the Newswire.com angle?

We have a partnership with Newswire.com that lets us submit press releases to their newsroom on behalf of clients with active listing subscriptions. Newswire is DA 70+, indexed by Google in 5-10 days, and actively cited by ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. It's a separate add-on at $250/post or monthly bundle pricing.

What's the verification process?

We verify business legitimacy before activating listings. Required items: business entity name and country of registration, links to active web presence, brief description of services, any relevant disclosure information (affiliate relationships, regulatory standing). Process typically takes 24-48 hours from application submission.

Can I cancel anytime?
